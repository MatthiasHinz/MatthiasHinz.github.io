About Me

My name is Matthias Markus Hinz. I recently graduated from the University with a master's degree in Geoinformatics and currently work as a scientific assistant with temporary employment. I am particularly interested in Geographic Information Systems (GIS), remote sensing, spatial statistics, data science and software development.

I am passionate about Geographic Information Science because it can make a positive impact on the environment and society.

At the moment, I am looking for new job opportunities that should match some of my interests mentioned above. For me, it is crucial to work within a supportive and professional team. Cooperating with national and international partners would a very appealing prospect. Please, do not hesitate to contact me if you think your company or organization is the right place.
